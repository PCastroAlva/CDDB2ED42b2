Sesión 2 – Vocabulary Expansion

En esta sesión verás diferentes imágenes de alimentos para ayudarte a aprender y recordar nuevo vocabulario en inglés. Observa cada imagen y piensa a qué grupo pertenece: frutas, verduras, cereales, lácteos o proteínas.


Grains are foods like bread, rice, pasta or cereals. They give your body energy to learn, move and stay active.

Dairy products are foods made from milk, like cheese, yoghurt or butter. They help your body grow strong and stay healthy.

Protein is found in foods like chicken, eggs, fish or beans. It helps your muscles grow and keeps your body strong.

Fish is a type of protein that helps your body stay strong. It is healthy and gives you important nutrients like omega‑3.

Vegetables are foods like carrots, broccoli or peppers. They give your body vitamins and help you stay healthy.

Fruit includes apples, oranges, bananas or grapes. Fruits are sweet, fresh and full of vitamins.
