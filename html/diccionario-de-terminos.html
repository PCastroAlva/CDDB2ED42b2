Diccionario de términos

Texto

En esta unidad vamos a aprender muchas palabras nuevas en inglés relacionadas con la alimentación saludable. Para ayudarte, hemos preparado este glosario: una lista de términos importantes con su significado explicado de forma sencilla.

Podrás usarlo siempre que lo necesites: para entender mejor los textos, para hacer las actividades y también para crear tus productos finales, como la infografía interactiva o el podcast/vídeo que harás al final de la unidad.

Este glosario es tu herramienta de apoyo. Está aquí para ayudarte a aprender, recordar y usar el vocabulario de manera segura y con confianza.

Balanced diet
Eating different types of food to stay healthy.

Breakfast
The first meal of the day.

Calories
Energy we get from food.

Carbohydrates
Foods that give us energy, like bread, pasta or rice.

Dairy
Food made from milk, like cheese, yoghurt or butter.

Fat
A nutrient that gives energy, but too much can be unhealthy.

Fruit
Food like apples, bananas or oranges.

Healthy
Good for your body.

Hydration
Drinking enough water.

Meal
Food you eat at one time (breakfast, lunch, dinner).

Minerals
Nutrients that help your body work well.

Protein
Nutrient that helps build muscles (meat, eggs, beans…).

Serving
The amount of food you should eat at one time.

Snack
A small amount of food you eat between meals.

Sugar
Sweet substance in many foods; too much is unhealthy.

Unhealthy
Not good for your body.

Vegetables
Food like carrots, lettuce or broccoli.

Vitamins
Nutrients found in fruit and vegetables that keep you healthy.

Water
The most important drink for your body.

Yoghurt
A creamy dairy food made from milk.

Zucchini
A green vegetable similar to a cucumber.
